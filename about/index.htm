Our Company

A Duo of Developers, Designing for Fun and for Glory

Neenjah Cupcakes is a software development team based in South San Francisco and Daly City, California, consisting of two newly-minted computer scientists, and we live and breathe ambition. It's who we are. As a couple of high school friends working to make an industry impact, we aspire for creative, novel, and intuitive solutions... and enjoying every second of it! We're both experienced programmers (mainly Java and C++), and absolutely thrilled about our new projects that will surely test our skills (old and new). Our goal is to develop products that embody that Neenjah Cupcakes brand of "Fun and Functional".
